Sister's Bakery

Nestled in the heart of the community, Sweet Crumbs Bakery is a warm, welcoming spot where the smell of fresh-baked bread, pastries, and cakes fills the air. Known for its handcrafted treats made with locally-sourced ingredients, Sweet Crumbs combines traditional baking techniques with a modern twist, offering everything from flaky croissants and buttery danishes to specialty cupcakes and custom cakes.
